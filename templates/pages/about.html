About STEM FORGE AFRICA LTD

Building a brighter future through STEM education

Who We Are

STEM FORGE AFRICA LTD is a professional, global organization dedicated to advancing STEM education across Africa. We are committed to providing world-class educational opportunities, fostering innovation, and developing the next generation of African scientists, engineers, and technology leaders.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we create platforms for young people to engage with cutting-edge technology, compete internationally, and develop the skills needed to thrive in the 21st century.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in Africa that empowers youth through STEM education, creating a generation of innovators who will solve global challenges and drive technological advancement.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality STEM education and competition opportunities that inspire, educate, and equip African youth with the skills and confidence to become global leaders in technology and innovation.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative thinking and problem-solving

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in everything we do

Global Impact

Making a difference on the world stage
